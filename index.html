About Me

Hi, my name is TJ and this is a site I built around one of my favorite childhood characters, Godzilla. If you don't know who Godzilla is then you've come to the right place. In short, he's a kaiju, which is genre of giant Japanese monster, and has become the pop culture icon for Kaiju worldwide. Godzilla originated from post-WW2 Japan as a symbol for the atrocity and destruction of the atomic bomb. Since then his role has evolved from symbol of destruction to various positions such as hero, villain, and most recently in his newest film as an environmental threat. Please feel free to browse my page for more information about Godzilla or myself!
